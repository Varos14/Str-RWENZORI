About the Rwenzori Mountains

The Rwenzori Mountains, also known as the "Mountains of the Moon", are located on the border between Uganda and the Democratic Republic of the Congo. This unique ecosystem is home to:

  • Africa's third highest mountain peak (Margherita Peak at 5,109m)
  • Rare and endangered species like the Rwenzori leopard and duiker
  • Unique vegetation zones including tropical rainforest and alpine meadows
  • Vital water source for millions of people in the region
  • Glaciers that have existed for thousands of years (but are rapidly disappearing)

Our Impact

Since 2015, our conservation efforts have:

  • Planted over 127,000 native trees
  • Trained 1,200+ farmers in sustainable agriculture
  • Installed 42 landslide warning systems
  • Protected 350+ acres of critical habitat
